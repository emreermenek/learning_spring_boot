spring.io sitesinden projects kısmından projelere bakarak öğrenebilirsin.

actuator/beans
List of all Spring beans registered with your application.
Spring Boot internal beans and also your custom beans for your app(@Component ec)
useful for debugging your app
*****
actuator/threaddump
List of all threads running in your application
Useful for analyzing and profiling your applicaion's performance
*****
actuator/mappings
List all of the request mappings for your app
Useful for finding out what request mappings are available
